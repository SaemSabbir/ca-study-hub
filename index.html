Principles of Taxation Income Tax Act 2023 (ITA 2023) • NBR Bangladesh
Chapter 1⭐ Foundation
Income Tax Act 2023 — Overview
ITO 1984 replaced by ITA 2023. Key changes & structure.

The Income Tax Act 2023

আয়কর আইন ২০২৩পুরনো ITO 1984 বাতিল করে নতুন আইন চালু
came into force on 22 June 2023 by gazette notification, replacing the Income Tax Ordinance 1984. The National Board of Revenue
জাতীয় রাজস্ব বোর্ড (NBR)কর প্রশাসনের সর্বোচ্চ সংস্থা
 administers this Act. Ref: ITA 2023

Key Changes from ITO 1984 → ITA 2023
🆕 New TIN (Taxpayer Identification Number) system
🆕 Refund of overpaid tax provisions added
🆕 Deregistration of WIN (Withholder Identification Number)
🆕 Tax holiday for newly established industries
🆕 Environment surcharge on multiple car owners
🆕 Revenue Policy Division (RPD) & Revenue Management Division (RMD) formed in 2025

⚠️ 2025 আপডেট

Finance Ordinance 2025 এ NBR ও IRD বাতিল করে দুটো নতুন সংস্থা গঠন: Revenue Policy Division ও Revenue Management Division। Ref: Finance Ordinance 2025

Chapter 2⭐ High Marks
Heads of Income — আয়ের উৎস
ITA 2023 এ কোন কোন উৎস থেকে আয় করযোগ্য
Heads of Income (ITA 2023)
📌 Total Income = Sum of all taxable heads
1️⃣ Salaries (বেতন) — Section 40 ITA 2023
Basic, allowances, perquisites, bonus, arrears, pension
2️⃣ Interest on Securities (সিকিউরিটি সুদ)
Govt/Non-Govt bonds and debentures
3️⃣ Income from House Property (বাড়িভাড়া)
Annual Value less 25% statutory deduction
4️⃣ Agricultural Income (কৃষি আয়)
Income from land used for agriculture in Bangladesh
5️⃣ Income from Business (ব্যবসায়িক আয়) — Section 45
Profits & gains from any trade, profession, or business
6️⃣ Capital Gains (মূলধনী লাভ)
Profit from transfer/sale of capital assets
7️⃣ Income from Other Sources (অন্যান্য আয়)
Dividends, royalties, prizes, gifts, lottery winnings
🧠 Mnemonic — 7 Heads মনে রাখো
S I H A B C O
Salaries · Interest on Securities · House Property · Agricultural · Business · Capital Gains · Other Sources

⚠️ পরীক্ষায় সতর্কতা

  • Royalty = Other Sources এর অন্তর্গত। Non-resident কে royalty দিলে 30% TDS Ref: ITA 2023 S.59
  • Stock dividend > cash dividend হলে 10% tax on stock dividend amount Ref: ITA 2023 S.23

    Chapter 5⭐ High Marks
    Investment Tax Rebate — বিনিয়োগ ছাড়
    Section 78 ITA 2023 — কীভাবে হিসাব করতে হয়
    Rebate Calculation — Step by Step Ref: ITA 2023 Section 78
    Step 1: Find Actual Investment Made
    ↓
    Step 2: Take LOWEST of 3:
    ① Actual investment
    ② 25% of Total Income
    ③ ৳1,50,00,000 (max)
    ↓
    Step 3: Rebate = 15% × (Lowest amount)

    📌 Eligible Investments (Section 78)

  • Life insurance premium
  • Provident fund contribution
  • DPS (up to ৳60,000/year)
  • Sanchayapatra / Savings certificates
  • Investment in listed securities (DSE/CSE)
  • Donation to approved national fund or charitable institution
    • 🧠 Rebate Formula
    15% of LOWEST
    Lowest of: Actual investment, 25% of income, ৳1.5 Cr max → ×15% = Rebate

    Contract Act⭐ Foundation
    Contract Act 1872 — চুক্তি আইন
    Valid contract এর ৭টি উপাদান + Void vs Voidable
    7 Elements of Valid Contract Ref: Contract Act 1872
    ✅ VALID CONTRACT — সব ৭টি থাকতে হবে
    1️⃣ Offer & Acceptance — প্রস্তাব ও গ্রহণ
    Offer must be definite. Acceptance must be absolute & unconditional (mirror image rule)
    2️⃣ Lawful Consideration — বৈধ বিনিময়
    Something of value exchanged. Past consideration = NOT valid in most cases
    3️⃣ Capacity of Parties — পক্ষের যোগ্যতা
    18+ years, sound mind, not disqualified. Minor's contract = VOID ab initio
    4️⃣ Free Consent — স্বাধীন সম্মতি (Section 14)
    No: Coercion, Fraud, Misrepresentation, Undue Influence, Mistake
    5️⃣ Lawful Object — বৈধ উদ্দেশ্য
    Not illegal, immoral, or against public policy
    6️⃣ Not Expressly Void — বাতিল নয়
    e.g. contract in restraint of trade = void (Section 27)
    7️⃣ Certainty & Possibility — নিশ্চয়তা
    Terms must be clear, definite and performable
    🧠 Mnemonic
    OACFLNC
    Offer · Agreement (Consideration) · Capacity · Free Consent · Lawful Object · Not void · Certainty

    ⚠️ Void vs Voidable vs Unenforceable

  • Void: No legal effect from beginning. Cannot be ratified. (e.g. minor's contract, unlawful object)
  • Voidable: Valid until avoided by aggrieved party. (e.g. coercion, fraud, misrepresentation)
  • Unenforceable: Valid but court won't enforce. (e.g. time-barred contract)
    • Companies Act📖 Important
    Companies Act 1994 — কোম্পানি আইন
    Types of companies, MOA, AOA, Incorporation process
    Types of Companies Ref: Companies Act 1994
    📌 Companies in Bangladesh
    🏢 Public Limited Company
    Min 7 members, no maximum. Can invite public to subscribe shares. Can list on DSE/CSE.
    🔒 Private Limited Company
    Min 2, Max 50 members. Cannot invite public. Share transfer restricted. Less compliance.
    🏛️ Company Limited by Guarantee
    Members guarantee to pay fixed amount if wound up. Common for NGOs/Associations.
    ♾️ Unlimited Liability Company
    Members have unlimited personal liability. Very rare in practice.

    📌 MOA vs AOA — Key Difference

  • MOA (Memorandum of Association): Company's constitution with the outside world. Contains: Name Clause, Registered Office Clause, Objects Clause, Liability Clause, Capital Clause.
  • AOA (Articles of Association): Internal rules for management. Contains: Meeting rules, Director powers, Dividend policy, Share transfer rules.
  • MOA supersedes AOA — In case of conflict, MOA prevails.

    ⚠️ Cheque Dishonour — Section 138 (Criminal)

  • Payee must give notice within 30 days of cheque dishonour
  • Drawer gets 30 days to make payment after receiving notice
  • If not paid → Criminal case → Up to 1 year imprisonment OR fine up to 3x cheque amount
  • Or both imprisonment + fine
